Our Story

Welcome to "GULLY BITES", where the vibrant spirit of street food meets the highest standards of hygiene and quality. Our story began with a simple yet powerful idea: revolutionising people's experience of street food. At Gully Bites, we believe everyone deserves to enjoy the mouth watering flavours of street food without compromising on cleanliness. We understand the hustle and bustle of urban life and aim to provide a quick, convenient, and safe dining experience for all.
